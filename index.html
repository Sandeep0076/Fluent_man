Motivation & Tips

Stay inspired on your journey.

The 80/20 Rule

Don't stress about perfect grammar. 20% of the vocabulary allows you to understand 80% of daily conversation.

Change Language

Change your phone's interface to German to learn practical, technical vocabulary passively.

Talk to Yourself

Narrate your day. "Ich mache jetzt Kaffee." It bridges the gap between thinking and speaking.

Media Consumption

Watch "Tagesschau" in 100 seconds. Listen to podcasts. Immersion finds the rhythm.

The "No English" Hour

For one hour, no English. If you don't know a word, describe it around the word.
