DeutschTagebuch

Your daily German learning companion—pirate-themed and playful.

What is this app?

DeutschTagebuch is a daily German practice app. Write short logs, get AI translations, collect vocabulary, and track progress across a 30‑day nautical journey. The One Piece vibe keeps it fun and motivating.

Main areas

  • Captain’s Deck — Your dashboard with streaks, stats, and the journey map.
  • Logbook — Daily journal. Write in English, translate to German, and refine.
  • Crew Chants — Helpful phrases. Add new ones directly from your writing.
  • Ship Notes — Personal notes and tips you want to remember.

How it helps

  • Practice writing daily with a lightweight, bullet‑style flow.
  • Get quick AI translations (English ↔ German) to learn naturally.
  • Tap German words to save them as phrases for later review.
  • Watch your progress grow on the 30‑day journey map.

Where your data lives

Data is stored in your connected Supabase project for persistence. On your device, the UI caches some state for a smoother experience. You can export or import your data from the app’s Data section.

Tips for getting started

  1. Open the Logbook and write 3–5 short bullets in English.
  2. Translate and edit the German version to match your style.
  3. Tap any interesting German word to add it to Crew Chants.
  4. Check Captain’s Deck to see your streak and map progress.

Questions or feedback

This is a personal learning tool. Feel free to tweak the theme, wording, or flow to fit your vibe.
